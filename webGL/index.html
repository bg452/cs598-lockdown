Instructions

    In this game your goal is to guide the blue civilian agents toward the green safety zones, while keeping them away from the red hostile agents. If an enemy comes into contact with a civilian, it's game over. You can place distraction cubes by left-clicking which will get the enemies' attention for a brief duration, or until they reach it. Additionally, using the WASD keys, you can place arrow signs on the ground which signal nearby civilians to proceed in the specified direction.

    Controls

    • W - places UP sign at cursor location
    • A - places LEFT sign at cursor location
    • S - places DOWN sign at cursor location
    • D - places RIGHT sign at cursor location
    • Left click - places DISTRACTION at cursor location
